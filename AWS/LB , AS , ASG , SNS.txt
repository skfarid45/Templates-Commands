Load Balancer :
+++++++++++++++
A load balancer accepts incoming traffic from clients and routes to EC2 instances(Targets).

The load balancer also monitors the health of its registered targets and ensures that its routes traffic on when the load balancer detects an unhealthy target, it stops routing traffic to that target.
It then resumes routing traffic to that target when it detects that the target is healthy again.

Types of loadbalancers -
4 Types -
========
1. Application LB
2. Network LB
3. Gateway LB
4. Classic LB
